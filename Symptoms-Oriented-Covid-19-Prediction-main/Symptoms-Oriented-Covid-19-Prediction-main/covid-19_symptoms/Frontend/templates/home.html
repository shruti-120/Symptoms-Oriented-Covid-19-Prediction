Why Try COVID Predictor?

Early diagnosis is crucial to prevent the development of the disease that may cause danger to human lives. This web application can be used to predict the presence of COVID-19 using symptoms.

COVID-19 Symptoms

COVID-19 affects different people in differnt ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

WHO Advice for the Public

Stay safe by physical distancing, wearing a mask, keeping rooms well ventilated, avoiding crowds, cleaning your hands, and coughing into a bent elbow or tissue.

WHO (COVID-19) Dashboard

The World Health Organization updates the public regarding the number of confirmed cases, death cases and total doses of vaccines administered.
